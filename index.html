If you want to share details about a missing person... Ever came across a situation, where someone that you knew went missing. Ever wished you could do more than file a complaint with the local cops. Here's a solution: Share details of the missing person with photographs, locate where the person went missing on a map, and share other relevant information places that the person knows, etc. This information is made available to everyone using our website which runs on desktop and mobile, and can be shared on social media like Twitter, Facebook, or over email, so that others can pitch in and assist in the search.

If you want to help in finding a missing person... Help others in tracking missing people by contributing a few hours of your precious time. Find missing people in your city and coordinate with others located near you to find missing people, organizing campaigns over Twitter and Facebook in their support, and share any leads (findings) with the community. Your effort will win you points and earn goodwill from others.

FAQ

How can I access information about missing people and things?
You can access this information using your Desktop or Mobile internet browser.
How can I collaborate with others using this app?
You can share information which may help others to locate the person or the thing. You can also find other people near you who may be interested, on a map, and team with them to solve a missing case.
Do you have a mobile app for your site?
We do not have a mobile app for this site, but it works just fine even in mobile browser. We may provide in the future based on end user need.
